Teruntuk Sayang Ku Novan

(HAPPY BIRTHDAY)

Dear Novan

Happy birthday, my love.

Time has been moving so slowly without hearing from you.

These past few months, we’ve rarely shared our stories, and in the silence between us, I’ve felt something missing.

I miss our simple conversations — the little things that used to feel so warm.

Today, I just want you to know… my wish remains the same:

may you always be happy, wherever your steps may lead.

Congrats Repeat Year, Novan <3

Jessica
